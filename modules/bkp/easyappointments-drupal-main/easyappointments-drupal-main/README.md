# Easy!Appointments Drupal Integration

> **Note:**
> This project is **not covered by Drupal’s security advisory policy.**

---

## Overview

Enhance your **Drupal** website by integrating the **Easy!Appointments** booking form directly into your pages.
Visitors will be able to schedule appointments **without leaving your site**, providing a seamless experience that increases conversions and engagement.

This module connects **Drupal** with your **Easy!Appointments** installation and allows you to display the booking form using a **custom Drupal block** that can be added anywhere on your site.

---

## Minimum Requirements

- **Drupal:** 10.0+
- **PHP:** 7.4+

---

## Connecting Easy!Appointments with Drupal

1. **Install and enable** the module from the Drupal admin panel.
2. Navigate to **Configuration → Easy!Appointments settings**.
3. Provide the **base URL** of your Easy!Appointments installation in the configuration form.
4. Once the connection is saved, the module will be ready to display the booking form through a custom block.

---

## Adding the Booking Form to Your Pages

After the module is enabled and configured, a new block named **“Easy!Appointments”** becomes available in the **Block Layout** section of Drupal.

To include the booking form:

1. Go to **Structure → Block Layout**.
2. Click **“Place block”** in the desired region.
3. Locate and select **“Easy!Appointments”**.
4. Optionally, adjust **visibility conditions** to control where the form appears.
